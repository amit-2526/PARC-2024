International Conference on Power Electronics & IoT Applications in Renewable Energy and its Control
(PARC 2024)

10 February, 2024



We are pleased to announce that the 2nd International Conference on Power Electronics & IoT Applications in Renewable Energy and its Control (PARC 2024) is being held at GLA University, Mathura, India from 10 February, 2024.
PARC 2024 is being organized under the aegis/ patronage of IEEE Uttar Pradesh Section. PARC 2024 aims to provide a platform for academicians, researchers and industrial participants alike to discuss the emerging trends and issues in grid interactive power converters, grid integration of renewable energy and IoT analytics in renewable energy sector.

ABOUT PARC



Prospective authors are invited to contribute high quality papers (4 – 6 pages) to PARC 2024 through the online submission system (EasyChair) in IEEE format. For more details please visit: PARC 2022
